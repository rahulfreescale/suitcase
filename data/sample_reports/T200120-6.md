# Respiratory Safety Pharmacology of BAY-7 in Dogs (Study T200120-6)

## Study Design
This respiratory safety pharmacology study evaluated a single intravenous dose of
BAY-7 in conscious Beagle dogs (4 males) at 0, 1, and 5 mg/kg. Respiratory rate,
tidal volume, and minute volume were recorded.

## Respiratory Observations
No effect on respiratory rate, tidal volume, or minute volume was observed at any
dose, including the top dose of 5 mg/kg.

## Conclusion
BAY-7 had no effect on respiratory parameters in dogs. The no-effect dose for
respiratory function was the highest dose tested, 5 mg/kg.
