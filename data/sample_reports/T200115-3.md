# Cardiovascular Safety Pharmacology of BAY-12 in Monkeys (Study T200115-3)

## Study Design
A single-dose cardiovascular safety pharmacology study was conducted in conscious
telemetered cynomolgus monkeys. BAY-12 was administered intravenously at 0, 3, and
10 mg/kg.

## Cardiovascular Observations
A mild, transient prolongation of the QT interval was observed at the top dose
(10 mg/kg), without accompanying arrhythmia. The effect resolved within a few hours.
No QT effect was observed at 3 mg/kg. Heart rate and blood pressure were unaffected.

## Conclusion
BAY-12 produced a mild transient QT prolongation at 10 mg/kg. The no-effect dose for
cardiovascular parameters was 3 mg/kg.
