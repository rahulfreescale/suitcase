# Single-Dose Toxicokinetics of BAY-1 in Rats (Study T123472-8)

## Study Design
This toxicokinetic study characterized the systemic exposure of BAY-1 after a single
oral dose of 100 mg/kg in Sprague-Dawley rats. Plasma samples were collected over
24 hours.

## Findings
No adverse clinical findings were observed following the single high dose. Systemic
exposure was dose-proportional relative to lower-dose reference data, with no
unexpected accumulation. Tmax occurred at approximately 2 hours.

## Conclusion
A single 100 mg/kg oral dose of BAY-1 was well tolerated with dose-proportional
exposure. No NOAEL-limiting findings were identified at this single dose.
